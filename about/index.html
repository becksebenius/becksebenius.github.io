Bio

Born and raised in the Snoqualmie Valley, I have enjoyed a life surrounded by the forefront of technological innovation and the beauty of the lakes and mountains of the pacific northwest. Through my work as a software engineer, I hope to promote positive change in the world and unlock the excitement and creativity that exists in each person.

Aside from my work as an engineer, my interests encompass a variety of other hobbies. I enjoy tinkering with hobby electronics and robotics, creating gadgets that can be used in daily life. I also dabble with making homebrew sour beers.
